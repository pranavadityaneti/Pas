2 · Your 11 decisions (recommendations in bold)

1. Tobacco / Paan Corner — Blinkit has Cigarettes (104), Cigar (14), Tobacco (5), Hookah (62), Lighters (110), Ashtrays (14), Rolling Needs (198). Recommend: exclude all tobacco/smoking SKUs; allow only Paan Masala + Non-Tobacco Blends under Grocery → General. (Aligns with the agreement-compliance gap that flagged no alcohol/tobacco controls.)
2. NULL category (325 items) — no Blinkit category at all. Recommend: route to an "Unmapped/Review" holding bucket, excluded from the storefront until classified. Do NOT auto-map.
3. Specials (Print-as-a-service, Bill Breaker, Freebie) — these are services/promo mechanics, not products. Recommend: exclude from the import entirely.
4. Digital Goods → E-Cards (629) — digital gift cards, not physical inventory. Recommend: exclude from the physical catalog (or a separate digital flow later).
5. Baby apparel — Blinkit "Baby Accessories & Apparel" (772) currently sits in Pharmacy → Baby Care. Recommend: keep all baby items together under Pharmacy → Baby Care for shopper convenience (vs splitting apparel to Fashion → Kids' Wear).
6. Body jewellery — Blinkit "Personal Care → Jewellery" (234) mapped to Fashion → Accessories & Jewelry. Confirm it's fashion jewellery, not body-piercing care.
7. Sports & Fitness vertical — Blinkit's sports/fitness items (nested in Home categories) routed to the standalone Sports & Fitness vertical via new "Sports, Fitness & Outdoors". Confirm.
8. Hardware & Plumbing vertical — Blinkit "Home Improvement" / "Hardware & Electricals" routed here (new "Home Improvement & Tools") rather than Home & Lifestyle. Confirm.
9. Pooja & Festive Needs vertical — currently no Blinkit category maps cleanly; its pooja/festive items were routed into Home & Lifestyle (Pooja Room Decor / Home Decor). Decide: leave Pooja vertical empty, or promote those items into it (would need new subcats there).
10. Frozen items — split: frozen savoury → Grocery (new "Frozen Foods"); ice cream / frozen desserts → Bakeries & Desserts. Confirm you don't want a single unified Frozen home.
11. "Paan & Tobacco" subcategory — held out of the seed entirely pending decision #1; nothing regulated gets created without your sign-off.

4 · Execution plan (each step ships + stops for approval)

  1. Schema: add a CategoryMapping table (blinkitCategory, blinkitSubcategory, pasVerticalId, pasTier2CategoryId, status: ACTIVE | PENDING_DECISION | EXCLUDED), unique on (category, subcategory). Migration — your approval before any DB change.
  2. Seed the 28 new subcategories under their verticals (idempotent upsert). Excludes the regulated "Paan & Tobacco" pending decision #1.
  3. Seed the mapping rows for the confidently-mapped categories (status ACTIVE), generated as a typed array reviewed against the CSV.
  4. Seed the decision-pending / excluded rows (NULL, Specials, Digital Goods, tobacco) as PENDING_DECISION / EXCLUDED — never hit the storefront.
  5. Wire the importer (feeds Phase 5 bulk-load): look up (category, subcategory) → assign vertical + subcategory if ACTIVE; else route to the Review queue. Log any unmapped pair.
  6. Tiny admin review screen listing non-ACTIVE + unmapped hits so you resolve the long tail one at a time.
  7. Verify: tsc after each step; dry-run a sample import and assert 0 products land with a null vertical and 0 storefront rows from excluded categories.

Drafted by a 7-agent mapping pass over the real 643-pair Blinkit taxonomy + your live 15-vertical / 95-subcategory taxonomy. Proposed new subcategories kept minimal and consistent with your existing naming. Nothing is seeded until you approve.
