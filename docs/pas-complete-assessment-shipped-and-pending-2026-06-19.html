PART 4 — Saved for Later / Pending

A. Legal & compliance (needs CA / lawyer — highest priority)

CRITICAL1. Re-draft the 3 merchant agreements

The in-app vendor agreements need a CA/lawyer pass on ~15 severe points — RBI payment-aggregation (flow of funds), the ECO GST/TCS/TDS framework, the "Lifetime Access" vs "onboarding fee" mismatch + no refund clause, unilateral commission changes, the 3-month-commission liability cap, one-sided indemnity, e-signature validity + Telangana stamp duty, FSSAI/drug-licence display, DPDP data-processing addendum, IP-licence-survives-termination, the 33→35 numbering gap + missing arbitration clause. (See merchant-agreements-severe-points-for-counsel-2026-06-19.html.)

CRITICAL2. Google Play rejection — login credentials

The current Play rejection (version code 15) is the App Access declaration: reviewers' test login didn't work. Add a working test merchant login + reviewer instructions in Play Console → App access, then re-submit. (Separate from the legal points; a quick fix.)

HIGH3. Commission & settlement mismatches (must fix before first real settlement)

(a) Agreements state a fixed % but admin sets commission_rate independently; (b) the commission engine's NULL-tier default (8%/10%) exceeds the signed restaurant/bakery rate (5%/7%); (c) Bakeries signed ₹2,999 but charged ₹999; (d) contract promises T+2 settlement but the system settles weekly. (Settlements are in dry-run — latent, not yet charged.)

HIGH4. Pharmacy & food licensing before those verticals go live

Pharmacy is currently routed to the generic template with zero drug controls (block or build an e-pharmacy schedule); FSSAI licence capture/display is optional today and PAS's own e-commerce FBO registration isn't addressed.

B. Finance / payouts

HIGH5. Real merchant payout formula (replace the ₹×0.98 placeholder)

Build the real settlement math — 1.9% payment-gateway fee + 18% GST on commission + 0.1% TDS (§194-O) + TCS — and a real "today's payout" endpoint. Gated: the 1.9% gateway fee is not authorised in the signed agreement text, so it can't be deducted until the charge is papered + re-consented (ties to A.1), and the math needs CA sign-off.

The KYC review works but: keeps no decision audit trail (who approved/rejected — compliance-critical), can't view the uploaded PDF documents, hides captured fields (veg/cuisines/bank/signature), has a cramped 340px decision panel, and an irreversible Approve with no confirm step.
